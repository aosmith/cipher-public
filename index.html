How it works

Install the app on your desktop or mobile device. On first launch, a 24-word recovery phrase is generated. All your keys are derived from this phrase, and messages are encrypted locally before being sent peer-to-peer.

Identity
24-word BIP39 recovery phrase generates deterministic Ed25519 keys
Encryption
XChaCha20Poly1305 with ephemeral X25519 keys for forward secrecy
Transport
Direct P2P connections using Iroh gossip protocol over QUIC
Storage
Local SQLite database on your device, never in the cloud
