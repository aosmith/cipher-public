How it works

Install the app and you're in — no email, no phone number, no sign-up form. Cipher generates a 24-word recovery phrase that becomes your identity, and everything you send is encrypted on your device before traveling peer-to-peer to your friends.

Identity
24-word BIP39 recovery phrase generates deterministic Ed25519 keys
Encryption
XChaCha20Poly1305 with ephemeral X25519 keys for forward secrecy
Transport
Direct P2P connections using Iroh gossip protocol over QUIC
Storage
Local SQLite database on your device, never in the cloud

Features

Serious cryptography, none of the homework

🔐

End-to-end encryption

Every message is sealed with XChaCha20-Poly1305 before it leaves your device. Only the people you send it to can read it.

🔑

No passwords, no accounts

Your 24-word recovery phrase is your identity and your keys. There's no password to leak and no account database to breach.

🌐

Peer-to-peer networking

Messages travel directly between devices over Iroh. There's no central server to breach, mine, or shut down.

🛡️

Forward secrecy

Every message uses fresh ephemeral keys, so past conversations stay private even if a key is ever compromised.

📱

Native performance

Built with Rust and Tauri — small, fast, native apps that feel at home on every platform.

⚡

Open source

AGPL-3.0 licensed and fully auditable. Don't take our word for any of this — read the code.
